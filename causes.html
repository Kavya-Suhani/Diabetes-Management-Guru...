    Causes of Diabetes

  • Overweight, obesity, and physical inactivity
  • Insulin resistance
  • Genes and family history
  • Hormonal diseases
  • Damage to or removal of the pancreas
  • Sometimes certain medicines can harm beta cells or disrupt the way insulin works.
  • Viral or bacterial infection
  • Chemical toxins within food and bad diet
  • Increasing age
  • The presence of damaging immune system cells (autoantibodies)
  • High blood pressure
  • Abnormal cholesterol
  • Environmental factors and geography
